About REVO TECH

REVO TECH is Galahitiya Central College's premier technology society, dedicated to fostering innovation, technical skills, and collaborative learning among students.

Our Mission

To empower students with technological knowledge and skills that prepare them for the digital future, while fostering creativity, innovation, and problem-solving abilities.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant community of tech-savvy students who will lead technological innovation and contribute to the digital transformation of society.

Our Journey

2018

REVO TECH was founded by a small group of tech enthusiasts at Galahitiya Central College with a vision to promote technology education.

2019

Organized our first coding workshop and hackathon, attracting participants from across the school.

2020

Launched the Technoriens annual technical investigation unit to promote research and innovation among students.

2021

Expanded our activities to include robotics and mobile app development, reaching over 100 active members.

2022

Won first place in the National School Technology Competition with our Smart School Assistant project.

2023

Established partnerships with local tech companies to provide internship opportunities for our members.

2024

Celebrating our growth with over 200 members and launching new initiatives to reach more students across Sri Lanka.

Our School

Galahitiya Central College is a prestigious educational institution committed to excellence in academics, sports, and technology.

Galahitiya Central College

Founded in 1952, Galahitiya Central College has been a beacon of educational excellence in Sri Lanka for over seven decades. Located in the serene surroundings of Galahitiya, our school provides a nurturing environment where students can excel academically and develop holistic skills.

The school has consistently produced outstanding results in national examinations and has a strong tradition of excellence in sports, arts, and technology. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing quality education and mentorship to our students.

Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

Mission

To provide quality education that develops intellectual, physical, social, and emotional capabilities of our students.

Technoriens

Our annual technical investigation unit dedicated to fostering research, innovation, and problem-solving skills among students.

What is Technoriens?

Technoriens is our flagship annual technical investigation unit that brings together students passionate about technology and innovation. Established in 2020, it provides a platform for students to explore cutting-edge technologies, conduct research, and develop solutions to real-world problems.

Each year, Technoriens focuses on a specific theme or challenge, encouraging participants to collaborate, think critically, and apply their technical skills to create innovative solutions. The program culminates in a showcase event where teams present their projects to peers, teachers, and industry professionals.

Technoriens 2024 Theme

"Sustainable Technology Solutions for Local Communities"
